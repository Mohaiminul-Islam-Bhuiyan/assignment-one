Our Donate Plan

We work in three different catagories. Food, shelter and education which are the fundamental needs of a human being, our goal is to ensure these three for all.

Food for a family

We are promised to provide necessary food items for the needy families around our working territory. we provide them not only food items but also medicines. Moreover we provide them baby foods too.

Shelter for a family

one of our fundamental duty is to provide shelter for the homeless people. we also work for victims of natural calamities. our duty is to provide them a minimum shelter as they can live with their family.

education for a child

As Education is the backbone of a nation, we work to ensure basic education for the children. our moto is to provide basic education for all the children. Morever we work for digital education system to prepare them for the modern world.
